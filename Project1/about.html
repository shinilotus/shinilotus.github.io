Founded in 2024, Build-A-Myth was started by the not-so mad scientist, Matt. Matt, growing up, had a distinct love for mythological creatures and was disappointed in the severe lack of availability in the world. Unsure if due to extinction or never existing in the first place, he sought out totally ethical genetic research and splicing to accomplish what the government likes to call "illegal" experiments.
